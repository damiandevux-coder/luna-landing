WHAT SHE DOES

The PM work that actually gets done

Not a chatbot that answers questions. An agent that runs your product ops.

📊

Know what's broken before standup starts

Every morning, Luna reads Linear and GitHub overnight. She surfaces the 3 things that need discussion, the 2 that don't, and the 1 that needs escalation. Your standup ends in 8 minutes. Your team actually knows what's happening.

🎯

A backlog that doesn't lie

Luna grooms daily. Missing acceptance criteria? Flagged. Stale for 2 weeks? Moved or killed. Duplicates? Merged. She RICE-scores everything and tells you what's actually ready for sprint. Your engineers stop picking up tickets that aren't tickets.

📄

Stakeholders who finally read updates

Luna auto-generates briefs with velocity trends, scope creep alerts, and a risk register written in English — not Jira-ese. Delivered as PDFs that look like you spent 3 hours on them. Because she did.

🔗

No more "I thought that shipped"

Luna links every PR to its ticket, checks CI status, and flags "merged but failing" before it hits staging. She knows what's deployed, what's broken, and who's fixing it. So you don't have to ask in Slack.

🚨

Blockers that die in 4 hours, not 4 days

Luna detects stalled work before it becomes a problem. She nudges assignees, suggests reassignments, and drafts escalation messages — all before your next standup. Blockers don't age. They get solved.

🔮

Know what they're building before they announce it

Luna monitors competitor moves, drafts battlecards, and alerts you to narrative shifts. Your next roadmap meeting starts with "Here's what they're doing" instead of "I think I saw something..."
