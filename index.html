FAQ

Questions you probably have

What is Luna, exactly?

Luna is an AI Product Owner that lives in Slack. She reads your Linear backlog, watches your GitHub repos, and posts daily briefs with what's blocked, what's ready, and what needs your attention. She also grooms your backlog, writes sprint briefs, and escalates blockers.

How is Luna different from ChatGPT or Copilot?

ChatGPT answers questions. Luna does the work. She doesn't just tell you what's blocked — she posts it to Slack, suggests fixes, drafts escalation messages, and tracks until it's resolved. She doesn't summarize your meetings — she prevents the problems that make meetings necessary.

What tools does Luna connect to?

Linear, Slack, and GitHub out of the box. Jira, Asana, Monday.com, and Azure DevOps on Pro and Enterprise. If your tool isn't supported, we build custom adapters.

Is my data secure?

Yes. Luna only reads the channels and repos you give her access to. Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We don't train on your data. SOC 2 Type II certified.

Can Luna make mistakes?

Luna is capable, not infallible. She double-checks her work and asks for confirmation before high-stakes actions like moving tickets to Done or escalating to executives. You stay in control.

What if I want to stop using Luna?

Remove her from Slack. That's it. No lock-in, no data hostage. Your backlog stays in Linear, your repos stay in GitHub. Luna just stops posting.
