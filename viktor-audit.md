# Viktor.com Landing Page — Full Design Audit

## Overview
Viktor positions itself as "Not a tool. A hire." — an AI employee in Slack/Teams. The page is a masterclass in product storytelling through visual design.

## Key Design Patterns

### Color Palette
- **Primary:** Purple/violet gradient (hero)
- **Secondary:** Peach/orange warm tones (hero accent)
- **Dark:** Navy/purple (CTA sections, buttons)
- **Light:** White, light gray (content sections)
- **Accent:** Green (saved badges), various for icons

### Typography
- **Font:** Modern geometric sans-serif (similar to Circular, Inter, or Geist)
- **H1:** 56-64px, bold, tight tracking
- **H2:** 36-48px, bold
- **H3:** 20-24px, semibold
- **Body:** 16-18px, regular, comfortable line height
- **Eyebrows:** 12-14px, uppercase, letter-spaced, muted

### Spacing
- **Section padding:** 80-120px vertical
- **Content max-width:** ~1200px centered
- **Card padding:** 24-32px
- **Generous whitespace** — never cramped

### Borders & Radius
- **Buttons:** Rounded-full (pill shape)
- **Cards:** 12-16px border radius
- **Inputs:** Rounded corners
- **Subtle borders:** 1px, low opacity

### Shadows & Depth
- **Cards:** Very subtle shadow (0 2px 8px rgba(0,0,0,0.04))
- **Floating elements:** Soft shadow
- **No heavy shadows** — clean, modern

### Animations (observed/inferred)
- **Text carousel:** Smooth fade/slide between phrases
- **Tab switching:** Content fade
- **Scroll:** Sections likely fade/slide in
- **Buttons:** Subtle hover lift/color change
- **Accordion:** Smooth height transition

### Visual Hierarchy
1. Hero: Biggest text, gradient bg, product preview
2. Problem statement: Large text, animation
3. Features: Visual previews + text
4. Comparison: Side-by-side contrast
5. Social proof: Quotes, photos
6. CTA: Dark background, bold text

---

## What Makes It Work

1. **Shows, doesn't tell** — Slack mockups everywhere
2. **Clear positioning** — "Not a tool. A hire." instantly differentiates
3. **Social proof layered** — Logos → testimonials → "ask AI"
4. **Comparison strategy** — Directly shows why competitors fall short
5. **Risk reversal** — Free credits, no card, SOC 2
6. **Visual rhythm** — Light/dark sections alternate
7. **Single focus** — Every section drives to "Get Started"
8. **Authenticity** — Real photos, real quotes, real app preview
